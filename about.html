About Cumilla Polytechnic Institute

Cumilla Polytechnic Institute is one of the leading government technical institutes in Bangladesh, dedicated to providing quality technical education and developing skilled manpower for the nation. Founded in 1962, CPI has a long history of producing innovative, disciplined, and job-ready graduates.

Our vision is to build a generation capable of meeting modern technological challenges through practical knowledge, creativity, and leadership skills. We aim to foster innovation and lifelong learning in our students.
