Textile Technology

About Textile Department

Textile Technology focuses on yarn manufacturing, fabric production, dyeing and garment processing. Students gain industrial knowledge of modern textile machines.

Courses we offer:

  • Yarn Manufacturing
  • Fabric Production
  • Dyeing & Printing
  • Garments Technology
